# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Be respectful

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, and abusive conduct are not tolerated.

## Reporting

If something happens that makes you feel unsafe or unwelcome, please open an issue or contact the maintainer.
